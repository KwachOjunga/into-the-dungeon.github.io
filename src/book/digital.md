## Constructs used in digital circuit optimization

- Commutative
$$
x . y = y . x
$$

$$
x + y = y + x
$$

- Associative
$$
x . (y . z) = (x . y) . z
$$

$$
x + (y + z) = (x + y) + z
$$

- Distributive
$$
x . (y + z) = x . y + x . z
$$

$$
x + y . z = (x + y) . (x + z)
$$

- Absorption
$$
x . (x + y) = x
$$

$$
x + x . y = x
$$

- Combining
$$
x . y + x . overline(y) = x
$$

$$
x + y . (x + overline(y)) = x 
$$

- De Morgan's Laws
$$
overline(x . y) = overline(x) + overline(y)
$$

$$
overline(x + y) = overline(x) . overline(y)
$$

$$
overline(x . y) = overline(x) + overline(y)
$$

$$
x . (overline(x) + y) = x . y
$$

$$
x  + overline(x) . y = x + y
$$

- Consensus

$$
x . y + y . z + overline(x) + z = x . y + overline(x) . z
$$

$$
(x + y) . (y + z) . (overline(x) + z) = (x + y) . (overline(x) + z)
$$
